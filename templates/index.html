About VigilEye

Protecting Digital Childhoods, One Alert at a Time

Every day in our world, millions of children navigate an increasingly complex digital landscape. While technology connects us like never before, it also exposes our most vulnerable family members to cyberbullying, online predators, and inappropriate content. Traditional parental control apps force families to choose between privacy and protection. We believe this choice shouldn't exist. VigilEye was born from a simple yet powerful idea: what if we could detect genuine online threats in real time while respecting children's privacy and building family trust? Our AI-powered platform analyzes digital conversations for signs of harassment, grooming, and explicit content, instantly alerting parents when their child faces real danger.

Our Vision

Today's prototype is just the beginning. We envision a world where children learn digital citizenship while staying protected, where parents have peace of mind without sacrificing family trust, and where technology serves families rather than exploiting vulnerabilities. As online threats evolve, so will VigilEye. Our roadmap includes voice and image analysis, multilingual support, and enhanced privacy controls. But our core mission remains unchanged: protecting what matters most while preserving the trust that makes families strong. Join us in building a safer digital future for every child.

Our Mission

We're reimagining child safety for the digital age. VigilEye combines enterprise-grade threat detection with a privacy-first approach, delivering proactive protection without the surveillance anxiety that plagues traditional monitoring solutions. Through intelligent alerts, educational features, and transparent operation, we help families navigate online risks together.

Our Features

Real-Time Intelligence

While other apps report incidents after harm occurs, VigilEye detects threats as they happen and immediately notifies parents with context and recommended actions.

Privacy by Design

VigilEye uses a “Kindred ID” created from non-identifying details like browser version and screen size so the system recognizes devices without storing names or contacts.

Educational Approach

Beyond monitoring, VigilEye teaches children about digital citizenship through gamified safety lessons and age-appropriate guidance.

Secure Messaging

All alerts and notifications between child and parent interfaces are encrypted end to end. Messages about potential threats travel through secure channels, guaranteeing that only authorized family members can read them.

Location Tracking

With explicit permission, VigilEye captures approximate device location only when a high-risk alert is triggered. Location data is shown in the parent dashboard in real time and then discarded, so no continuous tracking occurs.

App Usage Control

VigilEye lets parents set app-specific time limits and block or allow access as needed. Usage data is recorded only to enforce these rules and is not tied to personal identifiers, ensuring transparency and respect for privacy.

Cyberbullying Detection

VigilEye analyzes messages in real time to identify harassment, insults, or threatening language. When a potential cyberbullying incident is spotted, the system flags it immediately and notifies parents with context, helping address harmful behavior before it escalates.
